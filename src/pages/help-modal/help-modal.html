Welcome to Edesia, a food app! Slide the screen to the left to see how to use this app.

Add the ingredients that you have in your fridge, by clicking the left button from the bottom of the screen

After that, go to the Recipes page, by clicking the right button from the bottom of the screen

Now that you have the recipes, you can see the nutritional value of each recipe.

Press the get Recipes button to get the list of recipes.

Press each recipe to get a link to the cooking steps

Additionally, you can drag the pane from the bottom of the screen to see the nutritional information of each recipe.

You can always come back to this help by clicking the Options button (three lines) and click Help Page.
