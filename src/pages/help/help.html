How to use Edesia food app

Add the ingredients that you have in your fridge, or that you plan to buy.

Navigate to the Recipes page and based on your ingredients, search for an recipe. You will get recipe ideas and steps how to cook them. You can also store your preferences, so that you won't get suggestions of foods that you don't normally eat.

Now that you have the recipes, you can see the nutritional value of each recipe.
